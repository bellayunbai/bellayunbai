Welcome to the Intelligent Transportation Lab of NJIT

About Us

The Intelligent Transportation (INTR) Laboratory is a cutting-edge research facility within the Intelligent Transportation Domain at the New Jersey Institute of Technology (NJIT). The laboratory specializes in the modeling of multimodal and complex transportation systems, applying state-of-the-art data science, operations research, systems engineering, and management approaches to tackle challenges in transportation networks, supply chain optimization, and infrastructure asset management.

Research and Collaboration

INTR fosters a highly collaborative and interdisciplinary research environment, where students work alongside leading academics, peers, and industry experts in transportation engineering, artificial intelligence, optimization science, and more. The laboratory promotes international collaboration, supporting student participation in exchange programs and joint research initiatives with renowned institutions worldwide.

Industry Applications

The lab emphasizes industry-relevant research and technology transfer, integrating academic inquiry with practical applications. Students gain hands-on experience in product development, testing, and deployment, preparing them for careers at the intersection of academia, industry, and technological innovation.

Meet Our Professor

Professor Bai Yun leads the INTR Laboratory at NJIT. He specializes in transportation infrastructure management, health monitoring, safety perception, risk analysis, and network modeling and optimization. Professor Bai earned his Ph.D. in Civil Engineering from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign and previously served as an Assistant Research Professor at Rutgers University.
